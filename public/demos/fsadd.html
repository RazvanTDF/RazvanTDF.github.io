✦ Budget Wishlist App

A full-stack application for managing your wishes on a budget. Add products, track your spending, share lists with friends and get real-time notifications when someone buys you a gift.

React 19 TypeScript Vite Node.js Express MongoDB Socket.IO JWT Auth Recharts Swagger Jest
💰

Real-time budget

Track the total budget, the amount spent and the remaining amount, with a color-coded progress bar.

🧩

Sub-cost breakdown

Split an expensive product into components and check them off one by one as you buy them.

🔗

Sharing & QR code

Send your list to friends via a link or QR code; they can mark products as purchased.

🔔

Live notifications

Socket.IO instantly sends a notification when a friend visits your list or buys something.

📊

Statistics

Charts of monthly spending and the distribution of products by priority (Recharts).

🌙

Dark mode

Light/dark theme with automatic detection of the operating system preference.
